chocolate chip cookie recipe

ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter or margarine ,softened
  • 3/4 cupof sugar2 eggs,large
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla
  • 2 cups flour,unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups of warm milk

baking instructions

  1. heat oven to 375%.
  2. stir flour with baking soda and salt;set aside.
  3. in large mixing bowl beat butter wuth sugar ,add eggs and vanilla,one at a time. mix on low speed
  4. blend dry mixtures into created mixture
